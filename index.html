Track Suggester

To determine the Epicodus track that's right for you, answer a series of questions below:

What would you like to focus on?

Who would you like to work for?

What would you like to build?

What type of environment would you envision yourself in?

Which is most important to you?

Since you like the idea of working for a startup or smaller company building interactive web applications, the Ruby/Rails track is right for you.

Since you're interested in the front-end and on design focused websites, the CSS/React track is right for you.

Since you like the idea of working in a corporate environment building Windows desktop applications and focusing on the back-end, the C#/.NET is right for you.
